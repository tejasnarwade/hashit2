## 💳 EMERGENCY LOANS

### How Loans Work

**Automatic Loan Offer:**
- If an emergency cost exceeds your wealth, you're offered a loan
- Loan provides money immediately to cover the emergency
- You pay back the loan over 5 years

**Loan Details:**
- **Interest Rate**: 8% annual
- **Duration**: 5 years
- **Automatic Repayment**: Deducted from wealth each year
- **Total Cost**: More than borrowed (includes interest)

### Example: Medical Emergency Without Insurance

```
YEAR 2: Medical Emergency (₹50,000)
├─ Your wealth: ₹30,000 (not enough)
├─ Emergency cost: ₹50,000
├─ Shortfall: ₹20,000
│
├─ LOAN OFFERED: ₹70,000
├─ Interest: 8% per year
├─ Monthly payment: ₹1,167
├─ Annual payment: ₹14,000
├─ Total you'll pay back: ₹84,000
│
└─ NEW WEALTH: ₹50,000 (after emergency paid)

YEARS 3-7: Loan Repayment
├─ Each year: -₹14,000 (automatic)
├─ After 5 years: Loan paid off
└─ Total interest cost: ₹14,000
```

### Viewing Your Loans

**In Game Screen:**
- Scroll down to "💳 Active Loans" section
- See each loan with:
  - Principal amount
  - Interest rate
  - Years remaining
  - Annual repayment amount
  - Total cost with interest

**In Year Results:**
- "Loan Repayment" shown in calculations
- New loans highlighted with message
- Updated wealth after loan processed

### Best Practices

✅ **DO:**
- Buy insurance early to avoid loans
- Monitor your total debt
- Check analytics to see debt-to-wealth ratio
- Plan savings for emergencies

❌ **DON'T:**
- Ignore high debt ratio (>50% is risky)
- Rely on loans for all emergencies
- Take unnecessary risks with investments

## 📊 ANALYTICS DASHBOARD

### Opening Analytics

1. **Find the button**
   - Look for "📊 View Analytics" button
   - Located in the game controls section

2. **Click to open**
   - Modal opens with full analytics
   - Shows all game data in organized sections

3. **Close anytime**
   - Click the "×" button in top-right
   - Or click "Close Analytics" at bottom
   - Continue playing immediately

### Dashboard Sections

#### 🎮 Game Progress
- **Current Year**: Where you are in the game
- **Progress Bar**: Visual percentage of game completion
- Example: "Year 3 / 6" = 50% complete

#### 💰 Wealth Statistics
- **Starting Wealth**: ₹50,000 (always)
- **Current Wealth**: Your wealth right now
- **Wealth Gain/Loss**: Total change + percentage
- **Average Wealth**: Mean across all years
- **Wealth Chart**: Visual bar chart showing progression

#### 📈 Investment Allocation
- **Current Split**: Shows your 5 investment options
- **Percentages**: Each option's allocation (should total 100%)
- **Options**: Stocks, Gold, Silver, Commodities, Crypto
- **Visual Bars**: Color-coded allocation display

#### 🛡️ Insurance Coverage
- **Active Policies**: Count of active insurance types
- **Policy Types**: Medical, Accident, House
- **Coverage Status**: Which emergencies you're protected against
- Example: If 2 active, you're protected against 2 emergency types

#### 💳 Debt Management
- **Outstanding Debt**: Total amount you still owe
- **Debt-to-Wealth Ratio**: Debt as % of your wealth
  - 0-20% = ✅ Healthy
  - 20-50% = ⚠️ Manageable
  - 50%+ = ❌ High Risk
- **Loans Taken**: Total number of loans in this game
- **Interest Paid**: Total interest cost to date
- **Loan History**: Details of each loan taken

#### 💼 Salary Information
- **Current Salary**: Your annual salary
- **Annual Expenses**: 40% of salary (mandatory)
- **Available to Invest**: 60% of salary

#### 📋 Game Summary
- **Player Name**: Your name in this game
- **Progress**: Current year and years remaining
- **Final Status**: Wealth increased or decreased
- **Total Return**: Wealth gain as percentage
- **Insurance Coverage**: Number of emergency types protected
- **Debt Status**: Current debt situation

### Reading the Charts

**Wealth Chart:**
- Each bar = one year of wealth
- Taller bar = more wealth
- Can see exactly when wealth rose/fell
- Helps identify which decisions were best

### Strategy Insights

**Use Analytics to:**
1. **Monitor Wealth**: See if you're on track
2. **Track Investments**: Verify allocation working
3. **Plan Insurance**: Know what's protected
4. **Manage Debt**: Monitor loan repayment progress
5. **Optimize Strategy**: Adjust for future years

## 🎯 GAME STRATEGIES

### Strategy 1: Insurance First (Safe)
```
YEAR 1: Invest 100% in Gold (safe)
YEAR 2: Have insurance → Only pay 30% of medical
YEAR 3-4: Continue investing
YEAR 5: Have insurance → Only pay 30% of accident
YEAR 6: Have insurance → Only pay 30% of house

RESULT: No loans, high wealth ✓
```

### Strategy 2: Risk Management (Medium)
```
YEAR 1-4: Invest in mixed portfolio
YEAR 2: Medical emergency → Take emergency loan (unavoidable)
YEAR 3-5: Pay off loan while continuing to invest
YEAR 5-6: Buy insurance for remaining emergencies

RESULT: Some debt, moderate wealth △
```

### Strategy 3: High Risk (Aggressive)
```
YEAR 1: 100% Crypto (high returns, high risk)
YEAR 2: Emergency without insurance → Big loan
YEAR 3-7: Multiple loans, high interest costs
YEAR 8: High wealth but also high debt

RESULT: High returns but also high debt ✗
```

## 💡 TIPS & TRICKS

### Loan Math
- **8% interest** means:
  - ₹100,000 loan → ₹84,000 in interest over 5 years
  - Better to avoid if possible
  
### Debt Ratio
- **Safe**: Under 20% of wealth
- **Risky**: Over 50% of wealth
- **Check Dashboard**: See your current ratio

### Investment vs. Loans
- **Investment return**: -40% to +50% (variable)
- **Loan cost**: Exactly 8% (fixed)
- **Better to**: Get good investment returns than pay loan interest

### Insurance vs. Loans
- **Medical Insurance Cost**: ₹15,000 (5% salary)
- **Medical Emergency Loss**: ₹50,000
- **Insurance Saves**: ₹35,000
- **Lesson**: Insurance is cheap, loans are expensive!

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: What happens if I can't pay back a loan?**
A: Currently, loans are automatically deducted from wealth each year. Try to maintain positive wealth!

**Q: Can I take multiple loans?**
A: Yes! Each emergency can create a new loan if needed.

**Q: When do loans get paid off?**
A: Automatically over 5 years with annual deductions.

**Q: Can I see the full loan history?**
A: Yes! In the Analytics Dashboard under "Debt Management" > "Loan History"

**Q: What's the best Debt-to-Wealth ratio?**
A: Keep it under 20% if possible. 20-50% is manageable, 50%+ is risky.

**Q: Can I close analytics and open it again?**
A: Yes! Open and close as many times as you want during the game.

**Q: Do loans affect the leaderboard?**
A: Final wealth is used for leaderboard (after all debts), so loans lower your final score.
