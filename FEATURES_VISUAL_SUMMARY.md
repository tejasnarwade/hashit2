## 🎮 Example Gameplay Sessions

### Session 1: Conservative (No Loans)
```
YEAR 1: Invest in Gold (safe)
YEAR 2: Medical emergency → Have insurance → Pay 30%
YEAR 3-4: Continue investing in Gold
YEAR 5: Car accident → Have insurance → Pay 30%
YEAR 6: House emergency → Have insurance → Pay 30%

RESULT: No loans, ₹900,000 final wealth ✅
```

### Session 2: Risky (With Loan)
```
YEAR 1: 100% Crypto (high risk/return)
YEAR 2: Poor returns (-30%) → No insurance
YEAR 3: Recovery (+20%)
YEAR 4: Poor returns again (-25%)
YEAR 5: Car accident → No insurance + Low wealth
         → LOAN TRIGGERED: ₹120,000

YEARS 6+: Repay ₹28,800/year
RESULT: ₹600,000 final wealth, ₹120k debt ⚠️
```
